About the Project

This web app is designed to help users remain positive as they rank the activities in their day on a scale of 1 to 10 of positivity (1 is low positivity, 10 is high positivity). This lets users keep track of the events/activities that they enjoy. Over time, they can see their most popular ratings (of all of the events they've added), and how each event is rated in a monthly and yearly chart.
